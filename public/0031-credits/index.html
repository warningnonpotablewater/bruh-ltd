==============
Borscht recipe
==============

Serves

  • 4 people

Time to cook

  • 2 hours

Ingredients

  • Water
  • 0.5 kg pork bones
  • 2 medium carrots
  • 1 small beetroot
  • 1/2 of a medium onion
  • 4 medium potatoes
  • 1/4 of a small cabbage
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  • Boil pork (30 min)
  • Purify bouillon
  • Grate carrots and beetroot
  • Cut onion and cabbage
  • Chop potatoes
  • Fry carrots, beetroot, onion
  • Add bouillon, potatoes, cabbage
  • Boil for 1.5 hours
